# Journal of Biomedical Informatics Submission Checklist

Target journal: Journal of Biomedical Informatics

Recommended article type in Editorial Manager: Research Paper

Publishing route: Subscription / non-open-access option. This route has no publication fee charged to authors. Do not select open access unless Taipei Medical University confirms a full Elsevier waiver.

## Upload files

1. Manuscript: `manuscript.docx`
2. Title page: `title_page.docx`
3. Cover letter: `cover_letter.docx`
4. Graphical abstract: `graphical_abstract.png` or `graphical_abstract.pdf`
5. Declaration statements: `declarations.docx`
6. Statement of Significance, if the system requests it as a separate upload: `statement_of_significance.docx`
7. Suggested reviewers, only if the system asks: `suggested_reviewers.docx`

## Manuscript compliance

- Structured abstract with Objective, Methods, Results, Conclusion: 275 words.
- Main text from Introduction through Conclusion: 5,870 words.
- Required Statement of Significance is embedded in the Introduction.
- Body sections follow JBI structure: Introduction, Related Work, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion.
- Keywords: 7.
- Figures: 6.
- Tables: 2.
- References: 24.
- CRediT author contribution statement included in declarations.
- Generative AI use disclosure included in declarations.

## Cost reminder

Select the subscription publishing option unless a full open-access waiver is confirmed. JBI's open-access APC route is not free by default.
